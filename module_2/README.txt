The aim of the project is explore the influence of the living conditions of students aged 15 to 22 on their math performance in order to identify students at an early stage at risk.

Almost all columns in dataset have got empty values, but not many. Outliers are in the score values and absences values, mistakes are in 'fedu' and 'famrel' columns. So we can say that the data is clean enough.

Stages of work on the project:
1. Primary data processing.
2. Analysis of the distribution for the human variables, elimination of outliers.
3. Evaluation of nominal variables
4. Data transformation
5. Correlation analysis
6. Conclusions
